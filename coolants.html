SHIROKI metalworking fluids are engineered from decades of expertise in the design, development, and manufacturing of high-performance water-miscible cutting fluids and straight oils.

The SHIROKI METrange includes advanced water-miscible cutting fluids tailored for diverse machining applications. These formulations effectively regulate temperature at the point of friction, minimize tool wear, enhance chip evacuation, and deliver superior surface finish — ensuring precision, efficiency, and long-lasting performance in every operation.

Soluble Lubricants
Soluble concentrates with medium/high content of mineral oil or esters for versatile, high-lubrication operations.
Semisynthetic Lubricants
Low/medium mineral or ester oil content for balanced lubrication and cooling in general and demanding processes.
Synthetic Fluids
Water soluble compounds for high refrigeration requirements with excellent detergency — ideal for grinding and general machining.
Neosynthetic Fluids
Highly lubricating additives for severe operations requiring both lubrication and refrigeration with excellent detergency.
Auxiliary
Supplemental fluids and additives that enhance the performance and longevity of primary metalworking fluid formulations.

Soluble Lubricants

Soluble concentrates with medium/high content of mineral oil or esters. Versatile range and suitable for any type of operation, recommended when high lubrication is required such as in broaching, deep drilling and similar operations.

Semisynthetic Lubricants

Semisynthetic concentrates with low/medium content of mineral or ester oils. Recommended when lubrication and cooling functions are equally important, both in general machining operations and in highly demanding processes.

Synthetic Fluids

Synthetic concentrates based solely on water soluble compounds. Recommended when high refrigeration is required along with excellent detergency such as in general machining and grinding.

Neosynthetic Fluids

Water soluble concentrates with different contents in highly lubricating additives. Used when lubrication and refrigeration functions are equally necessary and when excellent detergency is desired even in the most severe operations.
