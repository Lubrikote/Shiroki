About Us

SHIROKI is a premier chemical manufacturing company with decades of experience in designing, developing, and manufacturing advanced industrial lubricants, metalworking fluids, and surface treatment solutions.

Our products are engineered to meet the most demanding performance requirements across a wide range of industrial sectors — from automotive and aerospace to heavy machinery and precision manufacturing.

Backed by a team of expert chemists and a state-of-the-art R&D facility, SHIROKI is committed to innovation, sustainability, and delivering exceptional value to our partners worldwide.

SHIROKI offers a comprehensive portfolio of high-performance chemical solutions — from metalworking fluids and industrial lubricants to advanced surface treatments and cleaners.

Every product is formulated with precision and backed by rigorous quality control, ensuring reliable performance that our customers can depend on in even the most challenging industrial environments.

Our range includes coolants, cutting oils, forming lubricants, hydraulic fluids, gear oils, chain oils, thermic oils, part protectors, cleaners, and auxiliary products — a complete solution for modern manufacturing.
