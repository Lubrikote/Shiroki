About Us

We are a specialized chemical solutions company dedicated to supporting modern manufacturing and industrial processes. Our expertise lies in post-treatment chemicals, cutting fluids, and impregnation chemicals, developed to enhance performance and efficiency across a wide range of applications.

With a strong focus on quality, consistency and innovation, we develop products that meet the demanding requirements of today's production environments. Our formulations are engineered to improve surface protection, machining efficiency, corrosion resistance and component reliability, while maintaining compliance with industry standards and environmental considerations.

We work closely with our customers to understand their specific challenges and operating conditions. This collaborative approach allows us to deliver tailored solutions, technical support and dependable supply, helping clients optimize processes, reduce downtime and extend product life. Driven by technical expertise and a commitment to continuous improvement, we aim to be a trusted partner for industries seeking reliable and high-performance chemical solutions.

At SHIROKI, performance is engineered with purpose. Built on decades of technical expertise in metalworking fluids and industrial lubricants, our solutions are designed to power precision, protect every surface, and optimize every process.

From cutting and forming to cleaning and protection, SHIROKI stands for reliability, innovation, and uncompromising quality. We don't just manufacture lubricants — we engineer efficiency, extend equipment life, and support sustainable industrial progress.

When performance matters, industries trust SHIROKI.
