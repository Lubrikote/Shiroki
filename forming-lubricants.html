Metal Working Fluids

Forming Lubricants

Catalog

SHIROKI solutions for metal forming applications are developed from decades of expertise in engineering advanced technical lubricants for the metal industry. Designed for extrusion, trimming, stamping, punching, and all types of forming operations, the SHIROKI range includes specially engineered medium- and high-pressure additive formulations tailored to each process.

These high-performance lubricants reduce friction coefficients, provide complete lubrication, and support consistent plastic deformation ensuring precision, durability, and operational efficiency.

Built with advanced formulation technology, SHIROKI forming lubricants optimize production processes, reduce waste, and help manufacturers meet stringent environmental and safety standards.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • High-performance, heavy-duty lubrication
  • Compliant with international environmental and safety standards
  • Reduced product consumption
  • Shorter cycle times
