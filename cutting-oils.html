Cutting Oils

Catalog

The SHIROKI cutting fluid range is formulated using highly refined mineral oils, synthetic oils, and premium-quality esters. These advanced formulations are engineered to enhance productivity, extend fluid life in circulation, reduce tool wear, and protect components, tools, and machinery from corrosion and rust ensuring consistent performance and operational efficiency.

Mineral Oils
Mineral oil base, suitable for any type of severity with low environmental impact and balanced composition.
Mineral-free
Biodegradable vegetal or synthetic oils — an environmentally friendly range based on non-polluting additives.
High Performance
Mineral oil base with high performance additives to lubricate at moderate temperatures, enabling lower energy consumption.
Auxiliary
Supplementary products designed for use in machinery to facilitate maintenance and cleaning operations.
